# 中文篇：Zhiné 全球品牌宪章

## 0. 文档定位

本文档是 Zhiné 的全球品牌总纲，用于统一品牌名称、品牌定位、核心叙事、产品类别、品牌人格、设计原则、品牌语气、命名体系和标语体系。

Zhiné 是唯一主品牌。对外传播、产品命名、技术文档、代码仓库、开放架构和社区表达，均应围绕 **Zhiné / zhine** 展开。

Zhiné 是一个开源项目。品牌表达、技术文档、代码仓库和社区材料应体现开放架构、开放协作和用户主权，不应采用封闭或保密项目口径。

本版本在保持既有品牌叙事连续性的基础上，将 Zhiné 的对外产品类别统一为：

> **Personal Intelligence Companion / 个人智慧伙伴**

技术层仍保留 **Personal Cognitive Model / 个人认知模型**、**Open Cognitive Architecture / 开放认知架构** 等表达。

---

## 1. 核心标语

> **不止于懂你。**

“不止于懂你” 是 Zhiné 的主标语。

它意味着：理解不是终点，而是起点。Zhiné 不只是理解用户的语言、意图和上下文；它还应记住、协作、保护、适应，并与用户一起成长。

英文主标语为：

> **Beyond understanding.**

---

## 2. 前言

人工智能已经进入一个由模型规模、推理能力和自主系统定义的时代。

但智能本身并不自动创造信任。

下一代人工智能之所以被铭记，不会只是因为它回答了更多问题，完成了更多任务，或调用了更多工具。

它会被铭记，是因为它让人的记忆、判断、经验和方法不再散落，并在长期关系中持续生长。

Zhiné 正是为这个未来而存在。

---

## 3. Zhiné 为何存在

纵观历史，每一次重大的技术革命，都扩展了人类能力的某一个维度。

- 车轮扩展了移动能力。
- 望远镜扩展了视觉能力。
- 计算机扩展了计算能力。
- 互联网扩展了沟通能力。

人工智能的真正意义，不是让机器更像人，而是让人突破自己。

今天，大多数 AI 系统被设计来回答问题、执行任务或生成内容。

很少有系统被设计为让一个人的智慧，不再散落于工具、平台与短暂对话之中，而成为随身生长的力量。

Zhiné 的诞生，是为了将 AI 从一次性工具推进为长期的个人智慧伙伴。

---

## 4. 我们的信念

我们相信，智能始于理解，但不止于理解。

理解语言还不够。

理解意图还不够。

记住上下文还不够。

真正值得信任的个人智能，应当能够在长期关系中学习、适应、协作、保护，并持续成长。

这正是 Zhiné 的核心信念。

---

## 5. 愿景

创造一种值得个人长期信任的 **Personal Intelligence Companion / 个人智慧伙伴**。

它不是另一个聊天机器人。

不是另一个搜索引擎。

不是另一个任务执行系统。

不是另一个 AI 外壳。

而是一个能够长期理解、记忆、协作、保护，并让个人智慧持续生长的智慧伙伴。

---

## 6. 使命

> **超越，才是自己。**

Zhiné 让每个人的智慧，不再散落于工具、平台与短暂对话之中，而成为随身生长的力量。

它服务的不是一次回答，而是一个人不断突破自己的长期旅程。

---

## 7. 品牌定位

Zhiné 开创并定义一种新的个人 AI 类别：

> **Personal Intelligence Companion / 个人智慧伙伴**

个人智慧伙伴是一种长期服务个人的智能系统。它能够：

- 理解个人上下文；
- 记住长期记忆；
- 与个人共同思考；
- 沉淀个人知识、经验和方法；
- 保护个人智慧资产；
- 与人协作，而非取代人；
- 随个人持续成长；
- 在不同设备和场景中保持连续可用。

对用户而言，Zhiné 是一个长期懂你、与你共同思考，并持续沉淀个人智慧资产的 AI 伙伴。

对开发者而言，Zhiné 是一套面向个人记忆、知识、经验、技能、评测、模型适配和成长闭环的开放认知架构。

## 8. 品牌哲学

### 8.1 理解是起点

每一次回应，都应始于理解。

但 Zhiné 不止于懂你。

理解之后，还要判断、协作、保护、记忆和成长。

### 8.2 共同思考

Zhiné 从不取代人的思考。

它与人共同思考，并延展人的判断能力。

### 8.3 共同成长

每一次互动，都应让个人智慧获得新的沉淀。

### 8.4 记忆创造连续性

没有记忆，就没有连续性。

没有连续性，就没有关系。

没有关系，就没有真正的个人智能。

### 8.5 信任先于智能

用户信任的是可理解、可解释、可治理的系统。

不是参数规模。

## 10. 设计原则

Zhiné 的所有产品、界面、文档和系统行为都应体现六项原则。

### 10.1 简洁

简洁的界面，呈现复杂的智能。

### 10.2 人性

技术应当让人感到被尊重，而不是被系统管理。

### 10.3 连续性

每一次互动，都应连接到下一次互动。

### 10.4 透明

用户应当理解系统为何如此行动、记住什么、忽略什么、何时需要确认。

### 10.5 进化

Zhiné 不应被设计成一次性工具，而应被设计成能够持续改进的个人智慧系统。

### 10.6 随身可用

Zhiné 不应被限定在某一台设备、某一个 App 或某一个平台中。

用户的个人智慧伙伴，应当能够在手机、电脑、浏览器、本地设备和未来智能终端之间保持连续体验，在用户需要时随时可用。

随身可用不意味着个人数据默认上传云端。它意味着在用户授权和治理边界内，个人智慧资产、身份、记忆索引和上下文状态可以安全同步、恢复和调用。

# English Version: Zhiné Global Brand Charter

## 0. Document Purpose

This document is the global brand charter for Zhiné. It defines the brand name, positioning, narrative, product category, brand personality, design principles, voice, naming system, and slogan system.

Zhiné is the single master brand. External communication, product naming, technical documentation, repositories, open architecture, and community language should all align around **Zhiné / zhine**.

Zhiné is an open-source project. Brand language, technical documents, repositories, and community materials should reflect open architecture, open collaboration, and user sovereignty, rather than a confidential or closed-project posture.

This version preserves the continuity of the existing brand narrative while updating the external product category to:

> **Personal Intelligence Companion**

At the technical level, Zhiné may continue to use terms such as **Personal Cognitive Model** and **Open Cognitive Architecture**.

---

## 1. Primary Slogan

> **Beyond understanding.**

“Beyond understanding.” is the primary slogan of Zhiné.

It means understanding is not the destination. It is the beginning. Zhiné does not merely understand language, intention, and context; it remembers, collaborates, protects, adapts, and grows with the individual.

Chinese primary slogan:

> **不止于懂你。**

---

## 2. Foreword

Artificial Intelligence has entered an era defined by model scale, reasoning capability, and autonomous systems.

Yet intelligence alone does not create trust.

The next generation of AI will not be remembered only because it answered more questions, completed more tasks, or called more tools.

It will be remembered because it helps people keep their memory, judgment, experience, and methods from becoming scattered, allowing them to grow over time within a long-term relationship.

Zhiné exists for that future.

---

## 3. Why Zhiné Exists

Throughout history, every major technological revolution has extended one aspect of human capability.

- The wheel extended movement.
- The telescope extended vision.
- The computer extended computation.
- The Internet extended communication.

The true meaning of artificial intelligence is not to make machines more human, but to help humans transcend their own limits.

Most AI systems today are designed to answer, execute, or generate.

Few are designed to keep a person’s intelligence from becoming scattered across tools, platforms, and fleeting conversations, and to turn it into a living force that grows with the individual.

Zhiné exists to move AI from a one-time tool into a long-term Personal Intelligence Companion.

---

## 4. Our Belief

We believe intelligence begins with understanding, but it does not end there.

Understanding language is not enough.

Understanding intention is not enough.

Remembering context is not enough.

A trusted personal intelligence should learn, adapt, collaborate, protect, and grow within a long-term relationship.

That is the belief behind Zhiné.

---

## 5. Vision

Create a Personal Intelligence Companion that individuals can trust over time.

Not another chatbot.

Not another search engine.

Not another task-execution system.

Not another AI wrapper.

An intelligence partner that understands, remembers, collaborates, protects, and lets personal intelligence grow with the individual.

---

## 6. Mission

> **To transcend is to become oneself.**

Zhiné exists so that personal intelligence no longer remains scattered across tools, platforms, and fleeting conversations, but becomes a living force that grows with the individual.

It is not built for a single answer, but for the lifelong journey of transcending oneself.

---

## 7. Brand Position

Zhiné defines a new category of personal AI:

> **Personal Intelligence Companion**

A Personal Intelligence Companion is a long-term intelligence system for the individual. It can:

- understand personal context;
- remember long-term memory;
- think with the individual;
- accumulate personal knowledge, experience, and methods;
- protect personal intelligence assets;
- collaborate rather than replace;
- grow continuously with the individual;
- remain continuously available across devices and contexts.

For users, Zhiné is an AI partner that understands them over time, thinks with them, and helps turn their knowledge, experience, and methods into personal intelligence assets.

For developers, Zhiné is an open cognitive architecture for personal memory, knowledge, experience, skills, evaluation, model adaptation, and continuous growth.

## 8. Brand Philosophy

### 8.1 Understanding Is the Beginning

Every response begins with understanding.

But Zhiné goes beyond understanding.

After understanding, it must reason, collaborate, protect, remember, and grow.

### 8.3 Growing Together

## 10. Design Principles

Everything Zhiné builds should reflect six principles.

### 10.1 Simplicity

Simple interfaces reveal complex intelligence.

### 10.2 Humanity

Technology should make people feel respected, not managed by a system.

### 10.3 Continuity

Every interaction should connect to the next.

### 10.4 Transparency

Users should understand why the system behaves as it does, what it remembers, what it ignores, and when confirmation is required.

### 10.5 Evolution

Zhiné should not be designed as a one-time tool, but as a personal intelligence system that improves over time.

### 10.6 Always Within Reach

Zhiné should not be confined to a single device, app, or platform.

A Personal Intelligence Companion should remain available across phones, computers, browsers, local devices, and future intelligent interfaces, while preserving continuity, privacy, and user control.

Always within reach does not mean personal data is uploaded to the cloud by default. It means personal intelligence assets, identity, memory indexes, and context state can be securely synchronized, restored, and invoked under user permission and governance.
