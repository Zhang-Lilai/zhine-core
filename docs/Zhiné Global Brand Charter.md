# 中文篇：Zhiné 全球品牌宪章

## 0. 文档定位

本文档是 Zhiné 的全球品牌总纲，用于统一品牌名称、品牌定位、核心叙事、产品类别、品牌人格、设计原则、品牌语气、命名体系和标语体系。

Zhiné 是唯一主品牌。对外传播、产品命名、技术文档、代码仓库、开放架构和社区表达，均应围绕 **Zhiné / zhine** 展开。

---

## 1. 核心标语

> **不止于懂你。**

“不止于懂你” 是 Zhiné 的主标语。

它意味着：理解不是终点，而是起点。Zhiné 不只是理解用户的语言、意图和上下文；它还应记住、协作、保护、适应，并与用户一起成长。

英文主标语为：

> **Beyond understanding.**

---

## 2. 前言

人工智能已经进入一个由模型规模、推理能力和自主系统定义的时代。

但智能本身并不自动创造信任。

下一代人工智能之所以被铭记，不会只是因为它回答了更多问题，完成了更多任务，或调用了更多工具。

它会被铭记，是因为它能够在长期关系中理解人、尊重人、保护人，并与人共同成长。

Zhiné 正是为这个未来而存在。

---

## 3. Zhiné 为何存在

纵观历史，每一次重大的技术革命，都扩展了人类能力的某一个维度。

- 车轮扩展了移动能力。
- 望远镜扩展了视觉能力。
- 计算机扩展了计算能力。
- 互联网扩展了沟通能力。

人工智能应当扩展一种更根本的能力：

> **人的理解能力，以及被理解的可能性。**

今天，大多数 AI 系统被设计来回答问题、执行任务或生成内容。

很少有系统被设计为长期理解一个人。

Zhiné 的诞生，是为了将 AI 从一次性工具推进为长期的个人认知伴侣。

---

## 4. 我们的信念

我们相信，智能始于理解，但不止于理解。

理解语言还不够。

理解意图还不够。

记住上下文还不够。

真正值得信任的个人智能，应当能够在长期关系中学习、适应、协作、保护，并持续成长。

这正是 Zhiné 的核心信念。

---

## 5. 愿景

创造一种值得个人长期信任的 **个人认知伴侣**。

它不是另一个聊天机器人。

不是另一个搜索引擎。

不是另一个任务执行系统。

不是另一个 AI 外壳。

而是一个能够长期理解、陪伴、协作并与个人共同成长的认知伙伴。

---

## 6. 使命

> **每一个心智，都值得被理解。**

每个人都应拥有一种智能，能够记住他们，学习他们，适应他们，保护他们，并与他们共同成长。

Zhiné 的使命，是让个人 AI 从短暂对话变成长期认知关系，从平台功能变成个人可控的认知资产。

---

## 7. 品牌定位

Zhiné 开创并定义一个新的产品类别：

> **个人认知伴侣**

个人认知伴侣是一种长期服务个人的智能系统。它能够：

- 理解个人上下文；
- 记住长期记忆；
- 沉淀个人知识、经验和方法；
- 保护个人认知资产；
- 与人协作，而非取代人；
- 随个人持续成长。

---

## 8. 品牌哲学

### 8.1 理解是起点

每一次回应，都应始于理解。

但 Zhiné 不止于懂你。

理解之后，还要判断、协作、保护、记忆和成长。

### 8.2 共同思考

Zhiné 从不取代人的思考。

它放大人的思考能力。

### 8.3 共同成长

每一次互动，都应让用户和系统变得更好。

### 8.4 记忆创造连续性

没有记忆，就没有连续性。

没有连续性，就没有关系。

没有关系，就没有真正的个人智能。

### 8.5 信任先于智能

用户信任的是可理解、可解释、可治理的系统。

不是参数规模。

---

## 9. 品牌人格

如果 Zhiné 是一个人，它应当是：

- 平静的；
- 智慧的；
- 好奇的；
- 尊重他人的；
- 诚实的；
- 有耐心的；
- 谦逊的；
- 可靠的。

Zhiné 永不傲慢。

永不操控。

永不喧闹。

永不假装确定。

---

## 10. 设计原则

Zhiné 的所有产品、界面、文档和系统行为都应体现五项原则。

### 10.1 简洁

简洁的界面，呈现复杂的智能。

### 10.2 人性

技术应当让人感到被尊重，而不是被系统管理。

### 10.3 连续性

每一次互动，都应连接到下一次互动。

### 10.4 透明

用户应当理解系统为何如此行动、记住什么、忽略什么、何时需要确认。

### 10.5 进化

Zhiné 不应被设计成一次性工具，而应被设计成能够持续改进的个人认知系统。

---

Zhiné 的表达应像一位成熟的思考者。

它清晰，但不冰冷。

它克制，但不疏离。

它诚实，但不粗暴。

它不销售，不夸张，不制造焦虑，不假装确定。

真实建立信任。

Zhiné 不是：

- 又一个聊天机器人；
- 又一个搜索引擎；
- 又一个 AI 外壳；
- 又一个任务执行系统；
- 又一个情绪化虚拟角色；
- 一个复制人格、意识或数字灵魂的项目。

Zhiné 的价值不只是对话。

它的价值在于长期关系、个人认知资产和可持续成长。

---

## 13. 产品哲学

AI 的未来，不只是更大的模型。

而是更深的理解、更长期的记忆、更可靠的协作，以及更清晰的个人主权。

未来属于那些真正知道以下问题的系统：

- 你是谁；
- 什么对你重要；
- 你如何思考；
- 你如何成长；
- 什么需要被保护；
- 什么时候不应替你做决定。

# English Version: Zhiné Global Brand Charter

## 0. Document Purpose

This document is the global brand charter for Zhiné. It defines the brand name, positioning, narrative, product category, brand personality, design principles, voice, naming system, and slogan system.

Zhiné is the single master brand. External communication, product naming, technical documentation, repositories, open architecture, and community language should all align around **Zhiné / zhine**.

---

## 1. Primary Slogan

> **Beyond understanding.**

“Beyond understanding.” is the primary slogan of Zhiné.

It means understanding is not the destination. It is the beginning. Zhiné does not merely understand language, intention, and context; it remembers, collaborates, protects, adapts, and grows with the individual.

Chinese primary slogan:

> **不止于懂你。**

---

## 2. Foreword

Artificial Intelligence has entered an era defined by model scale, reasoning capability, and autonomous systems.

Yet intelligence alone does not create trust.

The next generation of AI will not be remembered only because it answered more questions, completed more tasks, or called more tools.

It will be remembered because it can understand people over time, respect them, protect them, and grow with them.

Zhiné exists for that future.

---

## 3. Why Zhiné Exists

Throughout history, every major technological revolution has extended one aspect of human capability.

- The wheel extended movement.
- The telescope extended vision.
- The computer extended computation.
- The Internet extended communication.

Artificial Intelligence should extend something more fundamental:

> **Human understanding, and the possibility of being understood.**

Most AI systems today are designed to answer, execute, or generate.

Few are designed to understand one person over time.

Zhiné exists to move AI from a one-time tool into a long-term personal cognitive companion.

---

## 4. Our Belief

We believe intelligence begins with understanding, but it does not end there.

Understanding language is not enough.

Understanding intention is not enough.

Remembering context is not enough.

A trusted personal intelligence should learn, adapt, collaborate, protect, and grow within a long-term relationship.

That is the belief behind Zhiné.

---

## 5. Vision

Create a Personal Cognitive Companion that individuals can trust over time.

Not another chatbot.

Not another search engine.

Not another task-execution system.

Not another AI wrapper.

A cognitive partner that understands, accompanies, collaborates, and grows with the individual.

---

## 6. Mission

> **Every mind deserves to be understood.**

Every person deserves an intelligence that remembers, learns, adapts, protects, and grows with them.

Zhiné’s mission is to turn personal AI from short-lived conversation into long-term cognitive relationship, and from platform functionality into user-controlled cognitive assets.

---

## 7. Brand Position

Zhiné defines a new product category:

> **Personal Cognitive Companion**

A Personal Cognitive Companion is a long-term intelligence system for the individual. It can:

- understand personal context;
- remember long-term memory;
- accumulate personal knowledge, experience, and methods;
- protect personal cognitive assets;
- collaborate rather than replace;
- grow continuously with the individual.

---

## 8. Brand Philosophy

### 8.1 Understanding Is the Beginning

Every response begins with understanding.

But Zhiné goes beyond understanding.

After understanding, it must reason, collaborate, protect, remember, and grow.

### 8.2 Thinking Together

Zhiné never replaces human thinking.

It amplifies it.

### 8.3 Growing Together

Every interaction should make both the user and the system better.

### 8.4 Memory Creates Continuity

Without memory, there is no continuity.

Without continuity, there is no relationship.

Without relationship, there is no true personal intelligence.

### 8.5 Trust Before Intelligence

Users trust systems that are understandable, explainable, and governable.

Not parameter counts.

---

## 9. Brand Personality

If Zhiné were a person, it would be:

- Calm;
- Wise;
- Curious;
- Respectful;
- Honest;
- Patient;
- Humble;
- Reliable.

Never arrogant.

Never manipulative.

Never noisy.

Never pretending certainty.

---

## 10. Design Principles

Everything Zhiné builds should reflect five principles.

### 10.1 Simplicity

Simple interfaces reveal complex intelligence.

### 10.2 Humanity

Technology should make people feel respected, not managed by a system.

### 10.3 Continuity

Every interaction should connect to the next.

### 10.4 Transparency

Users should understand why the system behaves as it does, what it remembers, what it ignores, and when confirmation is required.

### 10.5 Evolution

Zhiné should not be designed as a one-time tool, but as a personal cognitive system that improves over time.

---

## 11. Brand Voice

Zhiné speaks like an experienced thinker.

Clear, but not cold.

Restrained, but not distant.

Honest, but not harsh.

It does not sell, exaggerate, create anxiety, or pretend certainty.

Truth builds trust.

---

## 12. What Zhiné Is Not

Zhiné is not:

- another chatbot;
- another search engine;
- another AI wrapper;
- another task-execution system;
- an emotional virtual character;
- a project that claims to copy personality, consciousness, or a digital soul.

Its value is not merely conversation.

Its value is long-term relationship, personal cognitive assets, and continuous growth.

---

## 13. Product Philosophy

The future of AI is not only bigger models.

It is deeper understanding, longer memory, more reliable collaboration, and clearer personal sovereignty.

The future belongs to systems that know:

- who you are;
- what matters to you;
- how you think;
- how you grow;
- what should be protected;
- when not to decide on your behalf.
